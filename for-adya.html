You're my words ki factory and I'm the guy who replies in three letters. So I sat down and wrote more than I've ever written in my life. Every line is true. Every line is you.

since when

It started with a random conversation on a campus walkthrough.
Then it was just mess, walks, and a Netflix password.
And then it was everything.

Back then I had no clue what you'd become to me. You were bossing me around about which girls I should and shouldn't talk to, calling me at hours where normal log so jaate hain, telling me to come down because "jaldi aa". I'd grumble. Then I'd go. Har baar.

Somewhere in all those walks and 2am calls you stopped being a friend and became the person — the one I'd save the day's best story for, the one whose "main boli thi na" I stopped fighting, because fine, you're always right. I gave up that argument a long time ago.

I kept it hidden behind short replies for way too long. Not today. Today you get all of it.
